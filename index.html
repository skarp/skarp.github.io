Hi! I'm a PhD student in the Machine Learning Department at CMU, where my research focus is deep learning theory. I am advised by Aarti Singh and often work with Yuanzhi Li.

I also work part-time at Google Research NYC, where my collaborators have included Satyen Kale, Pranjal Awasthi, Mehryar Mohri, and Behnam Neyshabur, among others.

Before grad school, I worked as a software engineer at Google NYC on search quality and the Google Assistant.

Before that, I was an undergrad at Princeton, where I studied theoretical computer science and worked with Robert Tarjan and Mark Braverman.
